ALAN WALKER

Alan Walker is a famous Norwegian-British DJ and producer known for his masked, hooded, anonymous persona, electronic music like "Faded," "Alone," and "Sing Me to Sleep," and his distinctive AW logo, blending genres like electro house, future bass, and progressive house to create melodic, high-energy tracks that built a global "Walker" community. He started making music in 2012 and rose to fame with "Faded" in 2015, becoming a major force in EDM with millions of streams and a strong online presence.
